Mission

Woody's Brewing Company - was founded with the belief that great beer can be brewed with local ingredients. We try to source 100% of our ingredients from local farmers and are focused on sustainable resources. Founder and Brew Master Shmen Shmoodard spent the last 7 years working with some of the best in the industry honing his craft. The beers he produces have inventive creativity, while upholding the most sound techniques and procceses to ensure the highest quality. We believe beer is a fantastic medium to bring people and local industry together.
